Pipelet UpdateStockLevel
Deprecated. Do not use this pipelet.
The pipelet increments or decrements the current stock level of the specified product by the specified quantity.

The pipelet ends on the ERROR exit if any of the following conditions are encountered:
- no inventory list is assigned to the current site
- no inventory record exists for the specified product in the site inventory list
- product inventory record has no allocation

Product bundles are handled specially: When the stock level for a product bundle is updated, the stock levels for the bundled products are updated accordingly. If a product bundle has its own inventory record, then the stock level for this record is also updated.

Input Parameters
Product  :  Product (Required)

The amount of the stock-level to decrease or increase. NOTE: If a positive number is provided the stock-level of the product is decreased. If a negative number is provided the stock-level of the product is increased.
